Liz received her BS from Florida International University, MS from Florida Atlantic University, and PhD from George Washington University. She was then a postdoctoral fellow at UCSD before joining the U.S. EPA. She has been affiliated with PEGL since her dissertation work, collaborating on projects related to the evolutionary and population genomics of sifakas.
