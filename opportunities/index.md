# {% include icon.html icon="fa-regular fa-envelope" %}Opportunities

Please email me to inquire about opportunities listed below.

## Postdocs

I don't currently have funding for a postdoc. However, I'm willing to work on funding applications with strong candidates. There are some internal funding opportunities at the University of Utah: [Wilkes Center Postdoctoral Program](https://wilkescenter.utah.edu/funding-opportunities/wilkes-center-postdoctoral-program/) (deadlines in January) and the [One-U Responsible AI Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://rai.utah.edu/opportunities/postdoctoral-fellows/) (deadline in April). However, most funding opportunities will be external, through the NSF, NIH, and various foundations. 

## PhD Students

I have funding for one or more graduate students to start in Fall 2027 (Dec 2026 application cycle). I accept students through the [Department of Anthropology at the University of Utah](https://anthro.utah.edu/).

## Undergraduates

I often have opportunities for undergraduates to conduct research in the lab. Most students are funded through the [UROP Program](https://our.utah.edu/research-scholarship-opportunities/urop/), but you don't have to be funded to start. Email me to schedule a meeting where we can talk about projects, funding, and how to get started.
